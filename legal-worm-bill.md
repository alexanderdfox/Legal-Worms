# Legal Self-Propagating "Worm Bill" Concept

This is a creative, fully legal design for a document that behaves like a benign computer worm: it encourages controlled, voluntary self-replication and spread through social and digital channels, **without malware, deception, or harm**. Think of it as a viral petition, meme, or awareness campaign wrapped in official-looking legislative language.

## Core Principles (to keep it 100% legal)

- **Explicit consent**: Every copy must state it is voluntary to duplicate and share.
- **No fraud**: No promises of money, prizes, or coercion.
- **Attribution**: Always include source, version, and contact.
- **Opt-out / destruction**: Include easy ways to stop the chain.
- **Platform rules**: Designed to comply with social media TOS, email policies, and anti-spam laws (e.g., CAN-SPAM safe harbor via clear unsubscribe).
- **Purpose**: Choose a positive, non-controversial goal (example below: "Right to Repair and Digital Freedom Bill").

### Section 1: Core Provisions (the actual "bill" content)

1. All devices and software sold must include user-replaceable batteries and repair manuals.
2. Manufacturers may not use software locks to prevent third-party repairs.
3. Right to data portability and interoperability between platforms.
4. Governments shall not mandate backdoors in consumer encryption.

- **Replication Rule**: Please create at least 3 copies of this bill (digital or printed) and distribute them within 7 days to friends, colleagues, online forums, social media, email lists, or public bulletin boards.

- **Payload Delivery**: Each copy carries the full text so it can spread independently.
